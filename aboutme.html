Reebal Bernadette

I used to work as a Lead Product Specialist, where I got really into analyzing my team’s performance with Excel formulas and creating weekly reports. That sparked a curiosity in me—what if I could dig into real-world data and make a meaningful impact? That led me to pursue a Master’s in Data Analytics at McDaniel College, Maryland, and along the way, I also picked up AWS certifications (CLF-C02 and SAA-C03) to dive into the world of cloud computing.

I love getting my hands dirty with data. Whether it's writing SQL queries, playing around with pivot tables in Excel, or visualizing insights in Tableau, I'm all in. I believe my mix of data skills and people skills makes me a great fit for roles in data analytics or engineering.

Thanks for checking out my story!
